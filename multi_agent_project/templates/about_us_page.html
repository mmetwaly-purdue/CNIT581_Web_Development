About Us

Discover who we are, our mission, and the team that drives us forward.

Our Mission

At Multi-Agent Sensemaking, we empower users to make sense of complex problems by creating AI agents that help organize, analyze, and connect information. Our goal is to give users the tools to visualize the relationships between their documents, aiding them in solving mysteries, making discoveries, or resolving challenges more effectively.

Our Vision

We envision a future where LLM Agents assists human problem-solving in a collaborative way. By integrating artificial intelligence into the process of document analysis and connection-making, we help users develop their own insights and build solutions, all while keeping them in control of the narrative.

How It Works

Users upload their documents to our platform, and by creating custom AI agents, they can extract relevant information from each document. Our system doesn't provide the solution but helps users build a story or a case, much like a detective assembling clues. The AI agents act as assistants, helping users connect the dots, visualize patterns, and make informed decisions.

Why Multi-Agent Sensemaking?

Our platform is designed with the user at its core. We believe that the most powerful solutions come from users who are able to harness technology in ways that empower their own decision-making. We aim to provide a platform that is not only easy to use but is a powerful tool in helping users discover the hidden connections within their data.
