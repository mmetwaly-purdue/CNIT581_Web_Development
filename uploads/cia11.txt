Report Date 27 April, 2003. CIA: From a laptop computer captured in Afghanistan it was learned that a Pakistani named Preston Burke, who fought with the Taliban in 1990 - 1992, travels using an Indian passport in the name Levi Schmitt. On this same computer was found the name Tom Koracick, who served with the Taliban from 1987 - 1993. Records on this computer reveal that Tom Koracick entered the USA in March, 1993 and uses the alias Atticus Lincoln.
